About Our Company

Deena's Garden & Synergy Ltd is a trusted agricultural company committed to providing quality seedlings, modern farming solutions, and professional agricultural support. Under the leadership of CEO Hajara Sulaiman, our company focuses on promoting sustainable agriculture through innovation, quality service, and customer satisfaction. We believe that every successful harvest begins with healthy seedlings and proper guidance. Our mission is to support farmers, organizations, and communities with reliable agricultural products and expert advice that improve productivity and contribute to food security. With dedication, integrity, and excellence, we continue to build strong relationships with our customers while creating a greener future for everyone.
